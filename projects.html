Personality Analyzer

A Flask/React app that profiles a Twitter user. Using a machine learning API, the app aggregates tweets from a given handle and outputs an analysis of their personality. Check it out with your own Twitter profile as well as with a big personality like @kanyewest. Increase the tweet count for a more representative analysis. Try it out here!

Soundcloud Likes

Another Flask app that tracks user's SoundCloud likes. It generates the perfect SoundCloud stream of songs you know you'll love by aggregating the likes of users you want to follow. Using SoundCloud's app, you can only view reposted tracks on your stream. This app allows you to follow the way most users acutally use the app. Give it a whirl!

Interactive Outerwear

For my Capstone project at NC State, I worked with a small team to develop a wireless enabled winter sports jacket. We built a custom Bluetooth unit that streamed music from a phone while displaying a colorful, interactive visualization along the hood.

The system was designed nearly from scratch and included a custom circuit to capture the music via Bluetooth and perform frequency analysis. It attached to and powered the jacket via conductive velcro and included a Lithium-Ion battery with charging circuitry.

To control the lights manually, I wrote a basic Android app that connected to the unit and delivered user input to it. It's default mode changed the light pattern and color based on the dominant frequencies in the music at that moment.

Our project won 1st place in its group.
