Soundcloud Likes

A Python-Flask app that tracks user's SoundCloud likes. It generates the perfect SoundCloud stream of songs you know you'll love by aggregating the likes of users you want to follow. Using SoundCloud's app, you can only view reposted tracks on your stream. This app allows you to follow the way most users actually use the app. Give it a whirl!

Interactive Outerwear

For my Capstone project at NC State University, I developed a musically reactive item of outerwear. A custom Bluetooth unit attached to the chest and streamed music to the user while displaying a visualization along the hood.

The whole system was designed from scratch and included a custom circuit with Bluetooth capabilities and spectrum analysis. It fit into a small enclosure and connected to the jacket via conductive velcor. I wrote an Android app to stream music from a user's phone to the jacket and control lights embedded within the hood. The default setting would visualize the music via the hood in a captivating way.
